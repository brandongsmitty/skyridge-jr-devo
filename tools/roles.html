How to Use These Descriptions

These are guidelines, not requirements. Every role owner has the freedom to develop systems that work for them, try new approaches, adapt based on what they learn, and make the role their own.

The only things we ask:

  • 1 Keep the head coach informed
  • 2 Communicate with your team
  • 3 Show up with energy and care
Everything else is yours to figure out. That's the point.
